# Privacy Policy for TRIO

**Last updated: February 2026**

## Overview

TRIO ("the App") is developed by Mert Can Cibuk. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App:
- Does NOT require user accounts
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your location
- Does NOT access your contacts
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- Does NOT share any data with third parties

## Local Storage

The App stores game progress (scores, settings) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us
- Is deleted when you uninstall the App

## In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases through Apple App Store and Google Play Store. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple/Google. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Children's Privacy

The App is safe for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.
